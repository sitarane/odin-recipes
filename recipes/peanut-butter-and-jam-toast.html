Peanut butter & jam toast

Description

Nothing beats this delicious tartine de confiture au beurre de cacahu√®te.

Ingredients

  • a slice of bread
  • two tea spoon of your favorite jam
  • two tea spoon of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Choose a slice of bread from the package, lay it on a small plate.
  2. Gently spread the peanut butter on top of the slice of bread. Pro-tip: use a rounded knife to spread it more evenly.
  3. Add the jam on top of the peanut butter. Pro-tip: don't go too close to the edges!
  4. Let it rest for at least ten minutes.
  5. Lay people use their hands, but I trust you to know better and use fork and knife like a respectable person.
