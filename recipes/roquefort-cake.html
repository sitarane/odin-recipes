Roquefort Cake

Description

There are few recipes in the world that have such an interesting difficulty to taste ratio as this roquefort cake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 200 g. white flour
  • 100 g. butter
  • about 14g. baking powder
  • 15 cl. milk
  • 125 to 150 g. roquefort
  • 50 g. parmesan
  • 2 table spoon honey
  • 12 nuts
  • a couple of raisins (optional)
  • pinch of salt (roquefort is already salty)
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 180Â°C
  2. At low temperature, gently melt the butter in a sauce pan
  3. Beat the eggs in a big bowl and add the flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper until combined
  4. Add honey in the sauce pan when the butter is melted
  5. Pour the content of the sauce pan into the bowl
  6. Cut the roquefort and the nuts in bits
  7. Gently add the roquefort, the nuts, and the parmesan in the bowl with the rest of the ingredients
  8. Put in the oven and bake for 45 minutes
